# Rajesh Hamal

## Overview
**Rajesh Hamal** is a legendary Nepali actor, environmental activist, and producer, widely regarded as one of the most influential and successful actors in the history of Nepali cinema. Often referred to as the "Maha Nayak" (Great Hero) of Nepal, he has dominated the Nepali film industry for over three decades.

## Personal Information

- **Full Name:** Rajesh Hamal
- **Date of Birth:** June 9, 1964
- **Age:** 61 years (as of 2025)
- **Place of Birth:** Tansen, Palpa, Nepal
- **Nationality:** Nepali
- **Occupation:** Actor, Producer, Environmental Activist
- **Years Active:** 1991 - Present
- **Spouse:** Madhu Bhattarai (married in 2014)

## Early Life and Education

Rajesh Hamal was born in Tansen, Palpa, Nepal to Dr. Hari Prasad Hamal and Droupadi Hamal. His father was a prominent physician and his mother was a housewife. He spent much of his childhood in Moscow, Russia, where his father was studying medicine.

### Education
- Completed schooling in Moscow, Russia
- Studied at Moscow State University
- Fluent in multiple languages including Nepali, English, Russian, and Hindi

## Career

### Early Career (1991-1995)
Rajesh Hamal made his debut in the Nepali film industry with the movie **"Yug Dekhi Yug Samma"** in 1991. The film was a commercial success and established him as a promising actor in the industry.

### Rise to Stardom (1996-2005)
During this period, Hamal became the undisputed superstar of Nepali cinema. His performances in various romantic and action films made him a household name across Nepal.

#### Notable Films:
- **Kasam** (1997) - Blockbuster hit
- **Deuta** (1997)
- **Chhadke** (1998)
- **Darpan Chhaaya** (2001)
- **Hami Teen Bhai** (2004)

### Continued Dominance (2006-Present)
Even after decades in the industry, Rajesh Hamal continues to be relevant and active in Nepali cinema, though he has become more selective with his roles.

#### Recent Notable Films:
- **Phagu** (2014)
- **Bir Bikram** (2016)
- **Gorkha Paltan** (2018)
